# News Sentiment Analysis

## Project Objective
**Client:** Nova Financial Solutions – Strategy Services Division.

**Two-Fold Analytical Mandate:**
1. **Large-Scale EDA:** To conduct a large-scale Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on financial news to identify patterns in publication frequency and recurring themes.
2. **Sentiment Analysis:** To perform sentiment analysis to quantify the emotional tone of market headlines and its potential correlation with stock volatility.

## Status
Task 1 and 2 completed.
